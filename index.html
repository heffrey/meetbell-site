meet bell

An app for practicing emotional honesty. Five modes that help you check in with yourself, name what you feel, and say what you mean. No accounts. No data stored. Just you and the question in front of you.

Inspired by the work of bell hooks.

The moment we choose to love we begin to move against domination, against oppression. The moment we choose to love we begin to move towards freedom.

bell hooks, All About Love

Five modes

Check-in

A daily question that cuts past "I'm fine." Answer honestly, get a brief reflection back.

Mirror

Talk about what's on your mind. bell listens and reflects back what she hears underneath.

Feeling

Can't name the emotion? Answer a few questions and bell helps you find the word for it.

Draft

Need to say something hard to someone? Write the raw version here. bell helps you shape it into something honest and clear.

Grow

Explore an emotional pattern or question in depth. bell draws on research and lived truth to help you see it differently.
