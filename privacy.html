Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

The short version

meet bell does not collect, store, or share your personal data. We don't have accounts, analytics, or tracking. Your conversations are not saved anywhere after they end.

What data we process

When you use meet bell, your text inputs are sent to our server to generate responses using an AI language model (Anthropic's Claude). These inputs are:

  • Processed in real time to generate a response
  • Not stored on our servers after the response is delivered
  • Not associated with any account or identifier
  • Not used to train AI models

What we don't collect

  • No names, email addresses, or account information
  • No device identifiers or IP address logging
  • No analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • No conversation history on our servers

Purchase validation

meet bell validates your App Store purchase receipt to confirm the app was legitimately purchased. This receipt is sent to Apple's servers for verification. We do not store receipts or purchase information.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party service to generate responses:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes your text inputs to generate responses. Anthropic's usage policy prohibits using API inputs to train models. See Anthropic's privacy policy.

Data stored on your device

Some responses and your check-in history may be stored locally on your device for your convenience. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Children's privacy

meet bell is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly process data from children.

Changes to this policy

If we make changes, we'll update this page. Material changes will be noted in the app.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@meetbell.app.
